Privacy Policy

Last updated on: 12 Mar 2026

TinyGoose Ltd (“TinyGoose”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from users of our applications ourselves.

  • We do not require user accounts
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact details
  • We do not collect analytics data
  • We do not use tracking technologies
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share user information

2. Data Stored on Your Device or iCloud

Some of our apps may store data such as settings, game progress, or high scores.

This data may be stored locally on your device and/or synchronised through Apple's iCloud Key-Value storage to allow syncing across the user's Apple devices.

TinyGoose Ltd does not have access to this data.

Any syncing is handled entirely by Apple as part of the user's iCloud account.

If you delete the app, this locally stored data may also be deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate third-party services that collect personal information.

However, when apps are distributed via Apple platforms, Apple may collect anonymised diagnostic and usage information (for example crash reports or analytics) as part of operating system or TestFlight functionality.

This data is processed by Apple and is not accessible by us except in aggregated or diagnostic form provided by Apple.

If this changes in future updates, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your country).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at the same URL with an updated effective date.
