Privacy Policy

Last updated on: 22 Feb 2026

TinyGoose Ltd (“TinyGoose”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

TinyGoose Ltd does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from users of our applications.

  • We do not require user accounts
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact details
  • We do not collect analytics data
  • We do not use tracking technologies
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share user information

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Some TinyGoose apps may store data locally on your device (for example: settings, game progress, or high scores). This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is not transmitted to TinyGoose Ltd
  • Is not accessible by TinyGoose Ltd

If you delete the app, this locally stored data may also be deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

TinyGoose apps do not integrate third-party services that collect personal information.

If this changes in future updates, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your country).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at the same URL with an updated effective date.
